Commercial Pages Ready To Publish

Page Published Content Operational Rule
Pricing Pricing and hardware plan covers Launch, Growth, Enterprise, setup fees, and reference hardware bundles. Quote only after confirming branch count, menu size, integrations, hardware, and support tier.
Support SLA Launch: next business day. Growth: 4 business hours. Enterprise: agreed response window, named escalation owner, and go-live support plan. Every pilot contract must include support owner, support hours, escalation contact, and production incident process.
Hardware Ownership Operators may bring approved hardware or buy a recommended bundle. Janova certifies exact model, connection method, and firmware per branch. No broad compatibility promise until the device appears in the lab matrix.
Payment Policy Cash works immediately. Card/wallet capture requires an active provider configuration, registered terminal device, provider reference, and settlement reconciliation process. No payment savings guarantee. Payment processing claims must match the signed acquirer/provider contract.
Data Ownership Owners can export products, customers, orders, payments, receipts, and inventory items as CSV from the owner app and API. Exports must remain available during subscription term and for a written offboarding period.
Cancellation And Offboarding Offboarding includes final CSV export, receipt archive handover, admin user shutdown, and hardware return or transfer checklist. Do not block customer data export for cancellation disputes.
Onboarding Discovery, menu import, branch setup, staff training, printer/terminal test, go-live rehearsal, and first-day support. Every go-live needs a signed fallback plan for internet, printer, payment, and staff login issues.
