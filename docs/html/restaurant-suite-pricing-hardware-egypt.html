Suggested Hardware Bundles

Small Cafe Bundle

  • 1 cashier station
  • 1 receipt printer
  • 1 cash drawer
  • 1 router
  • 1 UPS
  • 1 or 2 waiter tablets
  • 1 kitchen display tablet

Estimated greenfield hardware spend: about EGP 24,000 to 40,000 depending on printer and tablet choices.

Mid-Size Restaurant Bundle

  • 2 cashier stations
  • 2 receipt printers
  • 2 cash drawers
  • 1 router plus backup internet option
  • 2 UPS units
  • 3 to 5 waiter tablets
  • 1 or 2 kitchen displays

Estimated greenfield hardware spend: about EGP 55,000 to 110,000 depending on redundancy and tablet tier.

Practical Recommendation

Do not force every branch onto expensive all-in-one POS terminals on day one. In Egypt, a pragmatic rollout is usually better: stable Android tablets for waiter and kitchen, one dependable receipt printer, one cash drawer, good Wi-Fi, and UPS coverage for the cashier station and router.
