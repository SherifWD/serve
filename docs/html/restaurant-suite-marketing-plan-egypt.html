Marketing Plan To Launch In Egypt

This plan is based on the current product direction in this repo and current digital behavior data for Egypt.

Market Reality

DataReportal's Digital 2026 Egypt report says Egypt had 98.2 million internet users at the end of 2025 and 51.6 million active social media user identities in October 2025. That supports a launch plan built around Meta, short-form video, WhatsApp-based sales motion, and founder-led demos.

Target Segments

Independent Cafes

Fastest sales cycle, strong fit for Launch tier, very sensitive to onboarding simplicity and hardware cost.

Mid-Size Restaurants

Need waiter-kitchen-cashier coordination and ownership visibility. This should be the main initial revenue segment.

Small Chains

Best long-term logo value because branch comparison, SaaS control, and loyalty become much more important.

Positioning Message

  • Built for Egyptian restaurant operations, not just generic point-of-sale checkout.
  • Stronger floor operations UX: waiter, kitchen, cashier, and owner all have focused role workspaces.
  • More customizable than off-the-shelf suites when a branch has special service rules.
  • Customer layer is not an afterthought: history, branches, and points are built into the product story.

Channel Mix

Referral loop Low-CAC expansion Offer one free month or discounted implementation for every referred branch that goes live.

90-Day Launch Plan

Phase 1: Proof

  • Land 3 pilot branches.
  • Film real service walkthroughs using the waiter, kitchen, cashier, and owner apps.
  • Produce Arabic case-study pages focused on time saved, refund control, and branch visibility.

Phase 2: Pipeline

  • Run always-on Meta lead campaigns.
  • Use WhatsApp follow-up within 5 minutes for inbound leads.
  • Offer fixed-scope paid setup for operators who want fast launch with minimal custom work.

Phase 3: Expansion

  • Push multi-branch reporting and loyalty success stories.
  • Use founder content to position the product as the customizable Egypt-focused alternative.
  • Build a named implementation package for chains.

Phase 4: Brand Authority

  • Publish quarterly Egypt restaurant operations benchmark reports.
  • Host small breakfast events for owners and investors.
  • Turn best branches into video references and on-site tour references.

Recommended Monthly Marketing Budget

  • Starter: EGP 35,000 per month for one city focus.
  • Growth: EGP 75,000 per month for Cairo + Giza + Alexandria coverage.
  • Scale: EGP 150,000+ per month when you have strong case studies and a repeatable implementation team.

This budget guidance is a recommendation, not a sourced market rate. The exact split should be tuned after the first 30 days of lead quality data.

Success Metrics

  • Qualified demos booked per month
  • Pilot-to-live conversion rate
  • Average branch implementation time
  • Payback period on marketing spend
  • Referral share of new business
