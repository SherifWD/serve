Restaurant Suite vs Foodics

This view updates the earlier markdown comparison with the new Flutter applications and backend gap closures completed in this turn.

Current Position

The product is now stronger than the original repo snapshot because it no longer stops at backend-only dine-in operations. It now has runnable Flutter role workspaces for Customer, Waiter, Cashier, Kitchen, and Owner, plus a customer loyalty and order-history flow.

Where This Iteration Closed The Gap

Area What Was Added Here Why It Matters Against Foodics
Customer product layer Customer app, restaurants listing, order history, loyalty points, customer auth, customer linkage on orders Foodics is ahead in CRM and loyalty. This repo now has a real foundation instead of just data models.
Cashier maturity Split-tender capable payment flow and payment records Foodics explicitly offers richer dine-in payment handling; mixed tender removes a visible weakness.
Owner visibility Payment mix, branch performance, loyalty member count, KDS backlog, cashier queue This makes the ownership layer feel closer to an actual SaaS operator console.
Operational UI completeness Flutter workspaces for waiter, cashier, kitchen, and owner The previous repo state had backend capability without the matching mobile/operator product surface.

Where Foodics Still Stays Ahead

  • Wider packaged ecosystem: marketplace, broader integrations, and mature omnichannel layers.
  • Online ordering breadth: storefronts, delivery orchestration, and deeper ecosystem partnerships.
  • Enterprise packaging: more battle-tested onboarding, payments, and support motion across many branches.
  • Commercial polish: stronger breadth of polished operational edge cases out of the box.

Recommended Positioning

  • Position the product as the customizable Egypt-focused restaurant operating system, not as a total feature superset of Foodics.
  • Lead with table service UX, customer linkage, ownership visibility, and implementation flexibility.
  • Use Foodics as the benchmark to explain roadmap direction, not as a claim that the platform is already broader everywhere.
